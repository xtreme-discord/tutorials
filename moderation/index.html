1. What is Moderation ?

Controling your server and providing a safe environment to your members is known as Moderation.


Moderation in Xtreme includes many commands like purging, banning, kicking members, locking channels and manny more.

2. Getting Started

For getting a list of Moderation commands, type x!help moderation as shown in the image .


and for getting help on a specific command, type x!help {command name} like x!help kick . With this you are all set to use Xtreme Moderation in your servers.

3. Examples

As an example, to kick someone out of your discord server, type x!kick {member} as shown in the image and wow you have sucessfully learnt to use Xtreme Moderation in your server.
